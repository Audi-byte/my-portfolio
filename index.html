My Philosophy

Most training fails
before it starts.

Read My Story →

The problem isn't the authoring tool. It isn't the LMS. It isn't even the content.

The problem is that most organizations skip the most important question: Is this actually a training problem?

I start there. Every time. Only after diagnosing the real performance gap do I design a solution — and sometimes that solution isn't a course at all. It's a job aid. A decision tree. A manager conversation guide. A workflow change.

When training IS the right answer, I build it to work — grounded in cognitive science, designed for transfer, and built to be measured.
